Most Beautiful Places In The World

  • LONDEN

    London is the capital of the UK. It is a big city with the "River Thames" running through it. Famous places include **Big Ben, London Eye, and Buckingham Palace**. People from many countries live there.

  • THAJ MAHAL

    The Taj Mahal is in Agra, India. It is a beautiful white marble building built by Mughal Emperor "Shah Jahan" in memory of his wife "Mumtaz Mahal". It is one of the most famous monuments in the world and a **symbol of love**.

  • Sigiriya

    "Sigiriya" is in "Sri Lanka". It is an ancient rock fortress built on a huge rock. King **Kashyapa** made it in the 5th century. Sigiriya is famous for its **beautiful frescoes, gardens, and Lion Gate**. It is a **UNESCO World Heritage Site**.

  • Maldives

    The **Maldives** is a small island country in the **Indian Ocean**. It is made up of **many islands** with beautiful **white sandy beaches, blue seas, and coral reefs**. The Maldives is famous for **tourism, diving, and luxury resorts**.

  • The Grate Wall

    The "Great Wall " is in "China". It is a very long wall built long ago to protect the country from invaders. The wall stretches over **thousands of kilometers**. Today, it is a **famous landmark** and one of the **Seven Wonders of the World**.

  • Oia, Santorini, Greece

    **Oia** is a village on the island of **Santorini, Greece**. It is famous for its **white houses with blue roofs**, narrow streets, and beautiful views of the **sunset over the sea**. Many tourists visit Oia to enjoy its **scenery and charm**.
